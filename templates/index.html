How It Flows

One clean practice loop instead of scattered prep

Use the platform when you want each practice session to end with something useful: a score, a transcript, a delivery read, and a clearer next step.

01

Choose the right format

Start with a topic lane for repetition, a role round for breadth, or a custom plan when your interview already has a known structure.

02

Answer under light pressure

Speak through the round in one focused workspace so the session feels closer to a real interview than reading prompts from a list.

03

Review and adjust fast

Use the transcript, score, pacing, and report output to decide what to repeat next instead of guessing where you slipped.
