Notes

How to use this cleanly

  • Use Overview first to check whether users are active and where practice is concentrating.
  • Open Users to inspect who is active, how many sessions they have completed, and who has admin rights.
  • Open Sessions to spot recent interview activity and verify what people are actually running.
  • Create a topic first, then add questions under it.
  • Edit a role when you want to add an existing topic into its weight mix.
  • Use the weight inputs to decide how strongly a role should draw from each topic.
  • Question IDs are auto-generated for new questions, so you do not have to manage them manually.
